# Tips that I learned
## Photo Ops
Photos Ops are kinda underwhelming to me. I feel for the price of the picture doesn't live up to the value. A lot of the people who go offer the opportunites for a selfie at the table at a cheaper price so I think that is more appealing. You don't really get any interaction with the celebrity as it all goes so fast once in your in there. Sometimes you can take a long time waiting in line and that takes away your time from doing other things. 

## Autographs
I definitly think this is the way to go if you want a conversation with whoever you want to interact with. Every autograph I got came with a conversation. I got to ask the celebrities questions and just conversate with them. 

## Parking/Transportation
I would highly reccommend taking public transportation to the venue because parking can cost you quite a bit. On top of that especially on Saturday it can be heavily congested. It can take a while for you to get there.  

### Indies
I took interst in learning about indie games, both physical and digital. It was cool to hear the amount of work the creaters put in to get their finished product. 
### Artists/Authors
I love art because each person can draw the same topic very differently so I enjoyed walking around Artist Alley and looking at all of the different works of art. The different art styles and just talking to them about their work.
